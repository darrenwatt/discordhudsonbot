# Privacy Policy for Hudson Bot

**Last Updated:** June 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Discord application **Hudson** (ID: `462282642801295360`) handles, processes, and protects your information. We value your privacy and are committed to keeping your interaction with the bot secure and private.

---

## 1. Data Processing and Collection

### Message Content
* **What we process:** The bot processes the content of messages sent in Discord channels where it has access.
* **Why we process it:** This processing is done solely in-memory to detect specific keywords (e.g., `"alien"`, `"aliens"`, `"hudson"`, `"hicks"`) to trigger automated quote responses.
* **Storage and Retention:** **No message content is ever written to disk, stored in databases, or logged.** All message contents are immediately discarded after the keyword check is completed.

### Logging
* The bot logs its own internal events (such as successfully connecting to the Discord gateway, bot server count, and the quotes it broadcasts).
* These logs are printed directly to standard output for bot health monitoring and do not include user message history, user IDs, or any personal data.

---

## 2. Data Storage & Sharing
* **No Database Storage:** The bot does not maintain any database or persistent storage system.
* **No Third-Party Sharing:** We do not sell, trade, or share any user or message data with third parties.
